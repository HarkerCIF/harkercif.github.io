About The Harker School CIF

Harker CIF is a organization at The Harker School that focuses on proposing a humanitarian approach to nuclear disarmament and non-proliferation. Our mission, as defined by the MIIS Center for Nonproliferation Studies, is to prevent the proliferation of nuclear weapons with the eventual goal of nuclear disarmament.
